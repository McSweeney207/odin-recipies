Indian Chicken Curry (Murgh Kari)

Can't go wrong with this one!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Bread
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Cook Beans
  2. Cook Toast
  3. Butter Toast
  4. Put Beans on Toast

Alternativly, some people like to have their beans in a bowl and toast on the side as it prevents the toast from going sogy.
